About FabricFanatic

About Us & Our Skills

At Fabric Fanatic, we are passionate about fabric. We believe that the right fabric can transform a garment from ordinary to extraordinary. That's why we source our fabrics from the finest mills around the world and work with the most skilled artisans to bring our designs to life. Our team has a deep understanding of fabric properties and construction techniques. We use this knowledge to create clothing that is both comfortable and stylish. We also pay close attention to detail, ensuring that every stitch is perfect. When you shop at Fabric Fanatic, you can be confident that you are getting the highest quality clothing possible. We are committed to providing our customers with a unique and memorable shopping experience. Our Skills: Fabric sourcing and selection, Garment design and construction, Pattern making and grading, Sewing and finishing, Quality control.

FabricFanatic Services

Fabric Sourcing and Selection

We are experts Fabric care and maintenance and offer a wide selection of high-quality fabrics. We offer a wide selection of high-quality fabrics from all over the world. We have something for everyone, from basic cottons and linens to luxurious silks and wools. Our knowledgeable staff is always happy to help you find the perfect fabric for your next project.

Sewing and Finishing

We offer a variety of sewing and finishing services. We can sew your garment together, or we can simply finish your seam allowances. We also offer a variety of embellishment services, such as embroidery and appliqué.

Quality Control

We inspect all of our garments carefully before they leave our store. We want to make sure that our customers are happy with the quality of our work.
